Welcom to the CCycDB !

CCycDB is a knowledge-based functional gene database for accurate metagenomic profiling of carbon cycling microbial communities. CCycDB contains 4,572 genes with 10,548,067 targeted sequences involved in six modules covers 92 specific pathways.

Please see "" for more details. Please let us know if some genes have escaped our attention, we will be happy to add them !
